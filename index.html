Work Meter

Estimate the cost of electricity based on your computer's power usage and time.

How to Use

To use Work Meter follow these simple steps!

  1. Enter the power usage of your computer in watts (W).
  2. Enter the cost per kilowatt-hour (kWh) in USD.
    ℹ️ In the USA, this ranges somewhere from $0.15 - $0.20 per kWh.
  3. Press the "Start" button to begin tracking time.
  4. Press the "Pause" button to pause tracking.
  5. Press the "Reset" button to reset the timer.
  6. Press the "Stop" button to stop tracking and disable all buttons.
